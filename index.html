The Smith family is a very wealthy family that lives in a big, circular home. One morning, Mr. Smith woke up and saw a strawberry jam stain on his new carpet. He figured out that everyone who was there that morning had a jam sandwich.

Billy Smith: “I was outside playing basketball."
The Maid: “I was dusting the corners of the house."
Chef: “I was starting to make lunch for later.”

Correct!

The maid was lying. There are no corners in a circular house.
